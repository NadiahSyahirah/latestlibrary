Borrowing Guidelines

To ensure fair access to all resources and maintain a conducive learning environment, please observe the following guidelines:

  • A maximum of 3 books can be borrowed at one time per student.
  • The standard borrowing period for books is 2 weeks.
  • Please return books on or before the due date to avoid fines.
  • Fines will be charged for overdue books at RM0.10 per day.
  • Any damaged or lost books must be reported to the librarian immediately. Replacement costs may apply.
  • Handle all library materials with care to ensure they remain in good condition for future readers.
  • Food and drinks are strictly prohibited in the library area.
  • Maintain silence and respect the quiet atmosphere for studying and reading.
